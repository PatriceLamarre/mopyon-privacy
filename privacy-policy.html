Privacy Policy for MOPYON

Effective date: May 16, 2026

MOPYON is a real-time multiplayer strategy game. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and how users can contact us.

Information We Collect

MOPYON may collect and store limited gameplay-related information, including:

  • Player display name or nickname
  • Game room participation
  • Match results, wins, losses, draws, and leaderboard data
  • Basic technical information needed to support online gameplay

How We Use Information

We use this information to:

  • Enable real-time multiplayer gameplay
  • Display player profiles, rankings, and leaderboards
  • Save match results and game progression
  • Improve game stability and user experience

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may use trusted service providers to operate the app, such as backend hosting and database services.

Data Storage and Security

Gameplay data may be stored using secure cloud services. We take reasonable steps to protect the data used by the app.

Children’s Privacy

MOPYON is not specifically directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us so we can review and remove it if necessary.

Data Deletion Requests

Users may request deletion of their player data by contacting us at the email below.
